The company GreenTech Solutions was founded in 2015 and focuses on developing eco-friendly battery technology.
Their headquarters is located in Austin, Texas.
In 2022, GreenTech Solutions launched their flagship product, the EcoCell X, which can fully charge in 20 minutes.
The EcoCell X uses a proprietary lithium-silicon blend that increases battery lifespan by 40%.
In 2023, the company partnered with SolarWave Inc. to create solar-powered charging stations.
